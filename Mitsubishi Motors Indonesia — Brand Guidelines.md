# Mitsubishi Motors Indonesia (MMKSI)
## Brand Guidelines

---

## 1. Brand Identity

### Brand Name
**PT Mitsubishi Motors Krama Yudha Sales Indonesia (MMKSI)**
Commonly referred to as **Mitsubishi Motors Indonesia**

### Brand Essence
Mitsubishi Motors positions itself as a bold, adventure-driven automotive brand that empowers drivers to explore life confidently. The brand blends Japanese precision with the spirit of adventure relevant to the Indonesian market.

### Tagline
> **"Life's Adventure: Empowering Every Journey"**

### Supporting Brand Idea
> **"Drive Your Ambition"** *(Global Mitsubishi Motors positioning)*

## 3. Typography

### Brand Typeface
**fontsMMC** — Mitsubishi Motors' proprietary typeface

| Weight | Name | Weight Range | Style |
|--------|------|-------------|-------|
| Light/Regular | MMCRegular | 100–400 | Normal |
| Medium | MMCMedium | 500–600 | Medium |
| Bold | MMCBold | 700–900 | Bold |

**Format:** `.woff`  
**Fallback:** `Arial` (system font)

### Type Scale

| Role | Size (Desktop) | Size (Mobile) | Weight |
|------|---------------|---------------|--------|
| Hero Display | 86px–130px | 40px–50px | Bold |
| Section Heading | 40px–65px | 28px–35px | Bold |
| Sub-heading | 28px–32px | 22px–25px | Medium |
| Body Large | 20px–22px | 18px | Regular |
| Body | 16px | 14px–16px | Regular |
| Caption / Label | 10px–13px | 8px–10px | Regular |

### Typography Rules
- Use **MMCBold** for all headlines and CTAs
- Use **MMCMedium** for sub-headings, navigation, and UI labels
- Use **MMCRegular** for body copy and descriptive text
- Headlines: **ALL CAPS** or **Title Case** depending on context
- Never use fonts below 8px in any layout

## 4. Logo Usage

### Primary Logo
The three-diamond Mitsubishi mark is the core brand symbol. It must always appear in:
- **Red** (`#ED0000`) on white/light backgrounds
- **White** on dark/red backgrounds
- **Never** in any other color

### Clear Space
Maintain a minimum clear space equal to the height of one diamond unit on all sides of the logo.

### Logo Don'ts
- Do not stretch or distort
- Do not apply drop shadows
- Do not place on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
- Do not alter the diamond proportions

## 5. Imagery & Photography

### Hero Photography
- **Full-width, high-impact** vehicle photography
- Vehicles shown in **dynamic outdoor environments** — mountain roads, off-road terrain, urban cityscapes
- **Dark, cinematic tone** preferred for hero banners
- Aspect ratio: **16:9** for hero; **4:3** or **1:1** for product cards

### Lifestyle Photography
- Real Indonesian landscapes and settings
- Show adventure, family, aspiration
- People portrayed as confident, active, modern

### Vehicle Photography
- Clean studio shots with neutral or gradient backgrounds for product specs
- Action shots with natural environment for marketing campaigns

## 6. UI & Layout Principles

### Grid & Spacing
- **Full-width sections** for hero and campaign banners
- **12-column grid** for content areas
- Section padding: `80px` top/bottom (desktop), `40px` (mobile)

### Shapes & Geometry
- **Sharp angles and diagonal clips** are a signature element
  - e.g., `clip-path: polygon(30% 0, 100% 0, 100% 100%, 0 100%)`
  - Angled section dividers reinforce the dynamic, forward-moving brand personality
- **Minimal border radius** — sharp or very slightly rounded (`2px–6px`) for buttons and cards
- Avoid overly rounded, soft UI aesthetics

### Buttons

| Type | Background | Text | Border |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| Primary CTA | `#ED0000` | White | None |
| Secondary CTA | Transparent | White | `1px solid white` |
| Dark CTA | `#000000` | White | None |
| Outline (Light BG) | Transparent | `#ED0000` | `1px solid #ED0000` |

- Button text: **uppercase**, MMCMedium or MMCBold
- Hover state: darken background (e.g., `#bd0000` on hover for primary)

### Cards
- White background on light pages
- Dark (`#111010` or `#1a1919`) on dark/hero sections
- Thin border: `1px solid #e3e5e6` (light mode)

## 7. Tone of Voice

### Brand Voice Attributes
| Attribute | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Bold** | Confident, direct, never hesitant |
| **Adventurous** | Inspiring, forward-looking, energetic |
| **Trustworthy** | Reassuring, reliable, transparent |
| **Empowering** | Customer-centric, enabling, action-oriented |

### Writing Style
- **Headlines:** Short, punchy, action-oriented — 3–7 words
- **Body:** Clear, benefit-driven, avoids excessive jargon
- **CTAs:** Direct imperatives — *"Explore Now," "Book a Test Drive," "Learn More"*
- **Indonesian market tone:** Respectful but modern; blend formal and approachable

### Language
- Primary: **Bahasa Indonesia** for most consumer-facing content
- Secondary: **English** for technical specs, model names, and brand-level campaigns

## 9. Digital Brand Applications

### Website
- Dark hero sections with bold red accents
- White content areas for readability
- Sticky navigation with transparent-to-dark scroll behavior
- Red used sparingly — only for key CTAs and primary highlights

### Social Media
- Consistent use of MMC Red in graphics and borders
- Model names in MMCBold
- Campaign hashtags: clean, short, in Bahasa Indonesia or bilingual
- Vehicle imagery always hero-quality

### Email / eDM
- White background with red header bar
- MMC Red for CTA buttons
- Clean, single-column layout on mobile

---

## 10. Brand Don'ts

- Do not use the red on large background areas (except specific hero banners)
- Do not mix multiple accent colors — red or yellow, never both prominently
- Do not use competing typefaces alongside fontsMMC
- Do not use bright, playful or pastel colors — they conflict with the brand's bold character
- Do not use generic stock photography — always use contextually relevant, aspirational imagery
- Do not place logo or copy over busy image areas without a contrast overlay
